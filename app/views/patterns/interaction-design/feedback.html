Smart Survey

The welcome wizard pattern is a linear hub and spoke pattern.

It facilitates variable requirements and the option of retrospective changes. Unlike the Check your answers pattern the Welcome Wizard present 1 thing at a time and doesn't allow the user to choose which order to complete the steps. This lends itself to instances where later steps have dependencies on the pior steps.
